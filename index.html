    About Us

    Casachem Global Limited was incorporated on the 2nd of June 2021 in response to the need for Nigeria as a nation, to eliminate crude ethanol imports through enhanced cassava production and processing same into ethanol. Nigeria is the largest producer of cassava in the world, producing about 40 million tons annually, Cassava is one of the richest fermentable substances for the production of crude alcohol/ethanol.” – Foraminifera Market Research - however, the nation’s annual local demand for ethanol is between 300 and 400 million litres. The country only meets up with three percent of total annual demand. The balance of 97% is presently met through importation, requiring a whooping N160 billion – an expenses that must be curtailed.

    Our cumulative skills and knowledge allow us to be creative and innovative to deliver value adding products and services to our customers.
